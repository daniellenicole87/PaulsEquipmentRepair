ABOUT PAUL'S EQUIPMENT REPAIR

Paul has been the owner/operator of Paul’s Equipment Repair since 1993. His certifications in Maintenance and Repair from JLG, Genie, Ingersoll Rand, Lincoln, Generac, Brigss and Stratton, Kohler, Onan and Driver Training School, has both pushed and encouraged him to open his own business and better serve his community.

With experience of working on both large and small engines, hydraulics, pneumatics, drivetrains, electrical as well as preventative maintenance, Paul has worked to help his customers get back to their everday chores. Call today and be sure to get the best engine maintenance or advice in the area.
